## What LightAI adds vs. upstream Luma

- Built-in hardware-button remapping with single / double / long-press detection (no external Key Mapper needed)
- "Large buttons" home mode for the Light Phone III's squarish display
- AI prompt action: hand off prompts (text or voice) to the installed Claude Android app
- LP3-aware defaults for the side buttons, wheel, and wheel-click
